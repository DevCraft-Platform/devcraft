Our Story


DevCraft is born at 42 Málaga as the project of a student, Iván Novomiast
the idea was to create a platform where developers could share their projects, find work, learn new coding skill and connect with a bast community of developers.

The creator of DevCraft wanted to create a platform that could help developers to grow and improve their skills,
and that's how DevCraft was born, a platform that offers a lot of tools and resources for developers.

With DevCraft you'll be able to improve as a Dev, it doesn't matter if you are a beginner or an expert, you'll find something for you.
